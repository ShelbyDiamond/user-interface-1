I had a hard time coming up with ways to help out the community around me. This app reminded me how easy and simple a small act of kindness can go. Recently, my grand-puppy hurt his neck. My daughter took him to the vet, and he was put on 6 medications. The next day he (the dog) seemed to be very stressed out and in a lot of pain. While my daughter was in school, I took my grand-puppy to the vet for second opinion
Michael
I always thought it would be awkward to step in and help strangers. What if they didn't want my help? However, this app challenged me to push past my comfort zone and lend a helping hand. I saw an older lady struggle in the grocery store to reach her food item off the top shelf. Nervous she would reject my help, I offered anyway. She eneded up thanking me profusely and I was able to make her day a little bit easier.
